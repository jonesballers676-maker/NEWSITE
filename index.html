Our Materials

Premium Materials,
Built for Florida

We don't cut corners on materials. We partner with industry-leading manufacturers to give you the longest-lasting, best-looking roof possible.

Shingle Roofing

Atlas Roofing

For shingle roofs, we exclusively use Atlas Roofing products - including the Atlas Pinnacle Pristine and Atlas Select Pro lines. Atlas shingles are engineered for superior impact resistance, Florida heat, and come with up to a 50-year manufacturer's warranty.

  • Atlas Pinnacle Pristine - premium architectural shingles
  • Atlas Select Pro - 50-Year Warranty
  • Algae-resistant, impact-rated for Florida storms
  • Wide range of colors including Pewter, Charcoal & more
Metal Roofing

Premier Metal

For metal roofing, we install Premier Metal systems - the gold standard for durability in Florida's climate. Metal roofs last 40-70 years, outperform traditional materials by 3-4x, and provide superior protection against high winds, heavy rain, and fire.

  • Lifespan of 40-70 years with proper maintenance
  • Wind resistance up to 140+ mph - hurricane ready
  • Fire, mold, and algae resistant
  • Available in a wide range of finishes and colors
