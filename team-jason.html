About Jason

Jason Walton brings energy, expertise, and a genuine passion for helping people protect their homes to every customer interaction. As a Sales Associate with Robert Jones Roofing, Jason serves homeowners and businesses across Titusville, Merritt Island, and the broader Brevard County area.

Jason understands that a new roof is one of the most significant investments a homeowner can make — which is why he takes the time to explain all options clearly, from Atlas shingle selections to Premier Metal roofing systems, and helps customers understand their warranty and financing options.

If you're looking for a roofing professional who will be upfront, responsive, and genuinely invested in getting you the best result, reach out to Jason today.

What I Offer

  • Free, honest roof estimates with no pressure
  • Expert knowledge of Atlas shingles & Premier Metal systems
  • Guidance through the insurance claims process
  • Local expertise — I know Brevard County roofs
