Materials

About Our Materials

For shingle roofing, we exclusively install Atlas Roofing products - including the Atlas Pinnacle Pristine and Atlas Select Pro lines. Atlas shingles are engineered for Florida's climate, offer superior algae resistance, and come with warranties up to 50 years. We trust Atlas because we'd put their product on our own homes.
For metal roofing, we install Premier Metal systems. Premier Metal is one of the highest-quality metal roofing products available - built for Florida's heat, storms, and humidity. Premier Metal roofs last 40-70 years and are an excellent long-term investment for both residential and commercial properties.
Both are outstanding choices. Atlas shingles provide a traditional look, great curb appeal, and strong warranties at a competitive price. Premier Metal offers a longer lifespan (40-70 years), superior storm and fire resistance, and lower long-term maintenance costs. During your free estimate, we'll help you compare both options for your specific needs and budget.
Our Atlas Select Pro shingles come with a 50-year manufacturer's warranty. Premier Metal roofing also carries a long-term manufacturer's warranty. On top of that, we offer a 7-year workmanship warranty on every project we complete, and a 50-year warranty option is available on qualifying projects.
Florida-Specific

Florida Roofing Questions

Florida's intense sun, high humidity, heavy rainfall, and hurricane-force winds all accelerate roof wear. UV damage degrades shingles, humidity promotes algae and mold growth, and storm winds can lift or crack roofing materials. That's why we use Atlas Pinnacle Pristine (with built-in algae protection) and Premier Metal (rated for 140+ mph winds) for maximum Florida durability.
We recommend getting a free inspection in the spring - before hurricane season begins in June. This gives you time to address any issues before storms arrive. Our Roof Shield Protection Plan includes twice-yearly inspections to keep your roof in peak condition year-round.
Many Florida homeowner's insurance policies cover storm-related roof damage. Coverage varies by policy, so we recommend contacting your insurance provider. We're experienced in working with insurance claims and can help document storm damage to support your claim.
