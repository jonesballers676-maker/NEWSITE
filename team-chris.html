About Chris

Chris Threadgill is one of Robert Jones Roofing's most trusted Sales Associates, known throughout Brevard County for his straightforward, no-pressure approach. Customers consistently praise Chris for giving fair, competitive pricing and making the roofing process as easy as possible.

Chris takes pride in helping homeowners and business owners understand exactly what their roof needs - and why. Whether you're dealing with storm damage, a leaky roof, or planning a full replacement, Chris will walk you through every option honestly and help you choose the right solution for your budget.

With deep knowledge of Atlas Roofing shingles and Premier Metal systems, Chris ensures every customer gets the best material for their specific home, climate considerations, and long-term goals.

What I Offer

  • Free, honest roof estimates with no pressure
  • Expert knowledge of Atlas shingles & Premier Metal systems
  • Guidance through the insurance claims process
  • Local expertise - I know Brevard County roofs
